Support

Need help with Aqualytics? Here's how to get it.

Contact

The best way to report a bug or ask a question is to open an issue on GitHub:
github.com/dwcares/aqualytics-ios/issues

Requirements

Aqualytics requires a compatible Culligan Smart HE water softener with WiFi connectivity and a Culligan Connect account. Sign in with the same email and password you use for Culligan Connect.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't I see any data after signing in?
Make sure your softener is online and connected to WiFi, and that it appears in your Culligan Connect account. Pull down on the dashboard to refresh.
Where is my data stored?
Entirely on your device. Aqualytics has no servers and never uploads your data. See the Privacy Policy.
How do I enable tank tracking?
Open Settings inside the app and turn on Tank Tracking, then set your tank capacity.
How do I export my usage history?
On the Usage tab, tap the share/export button and choose CSV or PDF with your desired date range.
